COMPANY POLICY DOCUMENT - ABC Technologies

REFUND POLICY:
- Customers may request a refund within 30 days of purchase.
- Annual subscription refunds are prorated based on unused months.
- Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days.
- All refund requests must be reviewed and approved by a human supervisor.
- Refunds are issued to the original payment method.

SUBSCRIPTION CANCELLATION POLICY:
- Customers can cancel their subscription at any time.
- Cancellations take effect at the end of the current billing cycle.
- No refunds are issued for partial months on monthly plans.
- Annual plan cancellations require human supervisor approval.
- Data is retained for 30 days after cancellation.

ACCOUNT CLOSURE POLICY:
- Account closure requests must be submitted in writing (email or support ticket).
- All account closures require human supervisor review and approval.
- Data deletion occurs within 30 days of approved closure.
- Active subscriptions must be cancelled before account closure.

COMPENSATION POLICY:
- Service outages exceeding 4 hours qualify for service credit.
- Credits are applied to the next billing cycle.
- All compensation requests require human supervisor approval.
- Maximum compensation is 1 month of service credit.

SUPPORT POLICY:
- Standard support response time: 24-48 hours.
- Priority support for Enterprise customers: 4-8 hours.
- Support is available Monday to Friday, 9 AM to 6 PM IST.
- Emergency technical support available 24/7 for critical issues.

ESCALATION POLICY:
- Requests can be escalated to management upon customer request.
- All escalation-to-management requests require human supervisor approval.
- Escalations are handled within 2 business days.
